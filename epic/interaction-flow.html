Pre-condition

Must have an active Instagram account.

Post-condition

When completed user will successfully add a photo to their Instagram feed, and "like" a friends photo.

Add photo to Instagram Feed

  • Logs into account.
  • Access photo editor within app.
  • Uploads photo from personal gallery to app.
  • Edits photo.
  • Adds caption to photo.
  • Submits photo to her feed.

Like a friends photo

  • Log into account
  • Access Friends/Followers List.
  • Find desired friends profile.
  • Select friends profile link to gain access to their profile.
  • Locate photo.
  • Double click on photo to "like".
